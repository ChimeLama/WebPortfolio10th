#ALIVE

About the movie

While a grisly virus ravages a Korean city, Joon-woo tries to stay safe by locking himself inside his apartment. Just as he loses hope, he discovers another survivor.This is a korean movie and is available on Netflix with english subtitles.It was released in South Korea on June 24, 2020, and globally via Netflix on September 8, 2020.

Why this is one of my favorite movies.

I love this movie because it has an unexpected happy ending and i love how both of the main character help each other.
